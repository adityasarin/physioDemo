## 4. Review Strategy

The 747+ reviews at 5.0 are a moat. The goal is to:
1. Maintain consistent **velocity** (new reviews every week)
2. Maximise **keyword richness** in review text
3. Respond to every review within 48 hours

### 4.1 Getting New Reviews — The 3-Touch System

**Touch 1 — At checkout (highest conversion):**
Show the QR code (already printed — `images/google-review-qr.png`) on the billing desk or on a small card handed to the patient with their prescription/exercise sheet. Say: *"Sir/Ma'am, if you felt the treatment helped, a quick Google review takes 30 seconds and really helps other patients find us."*

**Touch 2 — WhatsApp follow-up (48 hours post last session):**
Send a personalised message via WhatsApp:
```
Hi [Name], hope you're feeling better after your session at our clinic.
Your feedback means a lot to us and helps other patients in need find relief.
If you have a moment, please leave us a quick Google review here:
https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=ChIJ01xYhInvDDkRUUbvKce4WFY

Thank you and wishing you continued recovery 🙏
— Dr. Wasim Raja Physiotherapy Clinic
```

**Touch 3 — For long-course patients (last day of treatment):**
At the final session, before the patient leaves, personally request: *"Your journey from [condition they had] to where you are now is exactly the kind of story that helps other patients trust that they can recover too."* Then share the QR code or link.

### 4.2 Review Velocity Target
| Period | Target New Reviews |
|---|---|
| Weekly | 3–5 |
| Monthly | 15–20 |
| Quarterly | 50–60 |

At 747+ reviews, maintaining velocity is more important than the count itself — Google's algorithm weighs recency heavily. A listing with 100 reviews from last month outperforms one with 700 reviews all from 3 years ago.

### 4.3 Responding to Every Review

**Never leave a review unanswered.** Google rewards engagement; patients read responses before booking.

**Template for 5-star reviews:**
```
Thank you so much [Name]! It means a great deal to hear that [briefly echo 
their condition/outcome]. We're glad the [TECAR/ShockWave/treatment] helped 
you get back to [what they mentioned]. Wishing you continued good health.
— Dr. Wasim Raja & Team
```

**Template for 4-star reviews:**
```
Thank you [Name] for your kind words and for trusting us with your care.
We're glad you're feeling better. If there's anything we can do to make
your next visit even better, please do let us know.
— Dr. Wasim Raja & Team
```

**Template for 1–3 star reviews (rare, but critical):**
```
Thank you [Name] for sharing your experience. We're sorry to hear your 
visit did not fully meet your expectations. We take all feedback seriously.
Please call us at +91 9013103095 or WhatsApp so we can understand what 
happened and make it right.
— Dr. Wasim Raja
```
> Never be defensive. Never mention the rating. Always take the conversation offline. A calm, professional response to a negative review converts undecided readers into patients.

### 4.4 What to Ask Patients to Mention in Reviews
Coach patients (naturally, not scripted) to mention:
- The specific condition they came in with
- The treatment used (TECAR, ShockWave, Myofascial Release)
- Time to recovery ("in 5 sessions", "within a week")
- Dr. Wasim's name

This builds keyword-rich review text which directly feeds the AI-generated summary and improves search relevance for those condition terms.

## 12. Risk Management — Protecting the 5.0 Rating

### 12.1 The Most Common Ways a 5.0 Drops
- **Slow review velocity** — older reviews get deweighted; rating can slip without new ones
- **Unanswered negative reviews** — one unanswered 1-star looks worse than five 1-stars that got professional responses
- **Incorrect hours** — if GBP says "open" but clinic is closed (public holiday, special occasion), patients arrive and leave a 1-star
- **Duplicate listings** — if a second, outdated listing exists for the clinic, it can confuse ratings

### 12.2 Pre-emptive Actions
- Update hours for all holidays and special closures **before** the day, not after
- Search Google for `"Dr Wasim Raja Physiotherapy"` and `"Mahagun Mart Physiotherapy Sector 78"` monthly — if duplicate listings appear, report them for removal via GBP support
- Set up a Google Alert for `"Dr Wasim Raja"` to catch any mentions or reviews on external platforms

### 12.3 If a Negative Review Appears
1. Respond within 24 hours — professionally, empathetically (template in §4.3)
2. Do NOT flag/report legitimate negative reviews — Google rarely removes them and the attempt is visible
3. Accelerate review requests to existing happy patients that week to dilute the impact
4. Never offer incentives in exchange for a review — this violates Google's policy and risks suspension
